<h4>What inspired this project and how was it developed...?</h4>
    <p>
        Being born on the Caribbean Island of Trinidad, I was used to Sunday market, where the farmers would gather and sell their natural, organic produce. Then, during my time in the USA, I noticed that I began developing allergic reactions to fruit, which I was used to eating. The uncertainty of the labeling of "Organic" is what sparked the inspiration behind this project. So, with my coding background, I'm now on a fuel to get this idea fulfilled of creating a self substainable farm, with the parameters of reducing the
        large scale footprint of farming..
    </p>
